2021 Georgia Congressional Plan

The Georgia Senate and House Committee Chairs (Senate Bill 2EX) passed this plan on 11/22/2021

##Redistricting Data Hub (RDH) Retrieval Date
01/05/2022

##Sources
This dataset was retrieved from the Georgia Legislative and Congressional Reapportionment Office at https://www.legis.ga.gov/joint-office/reapportionment

##Processing
The RDH retrieved the data from the source listed and did not modify any of the data.

##Additional Notes
Enclosed in this zip file the shapefile for Georgia's Congressional Districts ("CONGRESS-PROP1-2021-shape.shp") and supporting files, a PDF of summary statistics and maps ("congress-prop1-2021-packet.pdf"), and a report of counties within districts ("congress-prop1-2021-county-report.pdf").
